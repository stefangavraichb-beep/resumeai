Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

1. Who We Are

ResumeAI ("we", "us", "our") operates resumeai.today, an AI-powered resume optimisation service. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we use it.

Contact us at: support@resumeai.today

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you use ResumeAI:

  • Account information — email address, name, and password (stored securely via Supabase)
  • Resume content — the CV text you paste into our app (used only to generate your results and not stored permanently)
  • Job descriptions — the text you paste for optimisation (used only for the current session)
  • Usage data — how many optimisations you've run (stored locally in your browser)
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe; we never see or store your card details
  • Email address — if you opt in to our email list for bonus optimisations

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the resume optimisation service
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To send you product updates if you opt in (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve our AI models and service quality (anonymised, aggregated data only)
  • To process payments and prevent fraud

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your resume content to train AI models.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely on Supabase servers in the EU. Resume content you paste is processed in real time and is not stored in our database. We use HTTPS for all data transmission and apply industry-standard security practices.

Your browser stores your usage count and optimisation history locally via localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly submit it.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and account storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI processing of your resume (Privacy Policy)
  • Railway — application hosting (Privacy Policy)

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU or EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

To exercise these rights, email us at support@resumeai.today. You can also delete your account directly from within the app (Account → Security → Delete Account).

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for authentication (session tokens via Supabase). We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not use Google Analytics or similar third-party trackers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of ResumeAI after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at support@resumeai.today. We aim to respond within 48 hours.
