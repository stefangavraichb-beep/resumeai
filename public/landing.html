FAQ

Common questions

What is an ATS score? +
ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System — software used by companies to automatically filter CVs before a human reads them. Our AI scores how well your resume would perform against these systems and tells you exactly what to fix.
How does Targeted Application work? +
You type where you want to apply (e.g. "JP Morgan Spring Week London") and paste your CV. Our AI searches the web for that specific program, finds what they look for in candidates, then builds a fully tailored CV, cover letter, and interview prep pack from scratch.
Is my data safe? +
Yes. We don't store your resume content after processing. Your data is used only to generate your results and is never shared with third parties.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — cancel your Pro subscription at any time with no questions asked. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
Does it work for any industry? +
Yes. ResumeAI works for finance, tech, consulting, marketing, law, and any other industry. The AI adapts its language and focus based on the job description you provide.
